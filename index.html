Other data

WoK data: The bibliographic metadata for all journal articles published between 2011 and 2013 by authors at these 132 institutions (N = 347,880) in journals indexed by the Thompson-Reuters database (WoK).

  • Number of Deposits in Institutional Repositories

Publication date was estimated based on the Altmetrics database. When these data were not available, we used the WoK publication date and subtracted 5.26 months, which is the average difference in dates between Altmetrics and WoK. By subtracting the date when articles were deposited (as indicated by the IR metadata) from the date they were published, we calculated the Average Deposit Latency in months.

  • Deposit latency on Open Access and restricted access publications
